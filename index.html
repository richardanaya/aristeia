ARISTEIA

Rage of Diomedes

How to play

Click a tile to walk; two tiles away for Athena's leap (50 menos).

Moving past a foe cuts it; moving straight at one thrusts. Your first strike from a grounded spear sweeps an arc.

Ground the spear to stagger neighbors. Shield-bash foes into planks on fire. Guard turns arrows home. Libation for ford guards. Pray at Athena's statue for blessings.

Descend eight ranks, wound Ares, take your kleos, and flee for the ships.
